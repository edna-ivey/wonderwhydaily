## Body

Hi there,

Welcome to **Wonder Why Daily**.

This project started as a birthday gift for my son, Jonas.

On his 11th birthday, I wanted to give him something a little different.

Not another toy.\
Not another gadget.

I wanted to give him something that matched who he is.

Curious.

Like a lot of kids, Jonas notices things adults walk right past. He asks
questions that seem simple at first and then turn out to be surprisingly
difficult to answer.

Why do sharks never run out of teeth?

Why is space completely silent?

Why do fireflies glow?

He is always asking why.

Around the same time, I had already started another project called **Black
History In Real Time**. The idea was simple: instead of overwhelming people
with information, help them learn one small thing each day.

One story.\
One discovery.\
One step at a time.

It worked.

And I realized curiosity works the same way.

You don't need an hour-long lesson to learn something fascinating. Sometimes
all it takes is one good question.

So Wonder Why Daily was born.

Every day begins with a simple wonder.

A question.\
A guess.\
A discovery.

Because curiosity isn't something we outgrow.

It's how we continue to understand the world.

Each Sunday, you'll receive a roundup of the week's Wonders—a handful of
fascinating questions, surprising discoveries, and things you may never have
stopped to wonder about before.

Before you reveal an answer, take a moment to guess.

Ask your kids.

Ask a friend.

See who gets it right.

And most importantly...

Stay curious.

— Edna

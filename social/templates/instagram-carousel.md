# Instagram Carousel Template

## Slides

1. Hook: the Wonder question and an invitation to guess
2. Quiz: the three approved Wonder choices
3. Guess moment: a deliberate pause with no answer
4. Reveal: `shortAnswer` only
5. Second surprise: `coolFact`
6. One CTA: Stay Curious. Link in bio.

## Caption

Use the approved social hook, invite the reader to guess, show category and
rating, and close with `Stay Curious.` Do not reveal the answer or repeat the
CTA from slide 6.

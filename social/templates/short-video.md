# Short Video Template

## Script

1. 0–3 seconds: ask the Wonder question.
2. 3–8 seconds: show the three approved quiz choices.
3. 8–12 seconds: pause, ask which choice they would pick, and have them lock
   in an answer.
4. 12–20 seconds: reveal `shortAnswer`.
5. 20–30 seconds: add the independent `coolFact`.
6. Close with one CTA: Stay Curious and read the full Wonder.

Review pacing, factual accuracy, captions, and on-screen text before publishing.

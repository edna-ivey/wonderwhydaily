# Pinterest Pin Image Prompt

## Visual Direction

Wonder Why Daily editorial illustration style. Use dark forest green (#17221C), cream (#FFFAF0), and the Animals accent: #FFC46B mustard. Use large editorial typography, generous negative space, organic asymmetrical orbit lines, and one restrained orbiting dot. Use a simple paw-print or animal-track mark only when a category symbol helps the composition. The mood should feel observant, lively, and grounded in animal behavior. Timeless and curious, not childish, corporate, classroom-like, photorealistic, or visually noisy.

Create a 2:3 vertical editorial Pin designed for search and discovery.
Build a curiosity-first composition around "Why do bees dance?" and this hook: "A honeybee can return home and give the colony directions without drawing a map."
Use an intriguing unresolved visual rather than depicting the answer. Keep the title highly readable and leave breathing room around the illustration.
Do not include quiz choices, the correct answer, the short answer, or reveal imagery.

## Suggested Image Text

Why do bees dance?

## Alt Text Suggestion

Wonder Why Daily Animals illustration inviting readers to explore "Why do bees dance?" without revealing the answer.

## Safety And Review Notes

- Keep imagery safe and age-appropriate.
- Confirm the image creates curiosity without misleading the reader.
- Do not reveal or visually imply the answer.
- Review typography and contrast at mobile size.

## Status Definitions

- **Published:** The Wonder exists in the repository and its publication date
  is on or before the as-of date.
- **Scheduled:** The Wonder has an assigned publication date. It may already
  exist in the repository or may still require its repository content.
- **Approved:** Edna approved the title and category, but it has no publication
  date and no repository Wonder entry yet.

Only `Published`, `Scheduled`, and `Approved` are valid statuses.

## Editorial Rules

- Repository MDX frontmatter is authoritative for existing Wonder titles,
  categories, dates, and ratings.
- Edna's approved candidate pool is authoritative for Approved titles and
  categories.
- Approved scheduling decisions in this Bible are authoritative for Scheduled
  Wonders that do not have repository content yet.
- Ratings use repository frontmatter when it exists. Scheduled Wonders without
  repository content use editorial rating recommendations until their content
  is produced and reviewed. Approved candidates use `TBD`.
- Do not schedule, generate, or publish a title that is absent from this file.
- Do not add, rename, recategorize, or remove a Wonder without Edna's approval.
- A title must appear only once across all statuses.
- Moving an Approved Wonder to Scheduled requires assigning an approved date.
- A Scheduled Wonder requires validated repository content before publication.
- Moving a Scheduled Wonder to Published happens when its publication date
  arrives and its repository content exists.
